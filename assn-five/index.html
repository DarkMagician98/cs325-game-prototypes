  • High Concept: A clicking-game where your chances of winning lessens the more you click the wrong object.
  • Theme: I used the 'fame' theme. I just thought the more famous a person is, then the more followers he/she get. In this game's perspective, the more the player clicks the same object, the more chance the game will generate that object.
  • Mandated Variety: In terms of playstyle, it would be just players clicking on the right object to win. In terms of randomness, it would be based on chances.
  • Prototype Goal: Fast-clicking
  • Player Experience Goals: Intense
  • Gameplay: Players can click on the objects that moves around the screen--click the right ones and win, click the wrong ones and lose. The game generate a new object each time the player clicks on an object. The more the player clicks on the wrong ones, the less the right object generates.
  • Strategies: Play it over and over.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: None
  • Target Audience: Everyone
  • Play Time: 30 seconds per game
