  • Gameplay: The rules are simple: On each level, pick a number within 0 to 100--use a powerup, if need be, to narrow down possible answers. Players can play the game several times, but it goes back to level 0 at every start. However, they cannot retain their scores after a game is over. Simultaneously guessing the correct number will open up a multiplier that doubles the player's score per level after the opening. The chances of getting a powerup also increase as the streak goes up. The player starts with three unique powerups--30% within, divisible by n, and even/odd. "30% within" is a powerup where players can narrow down the answer to 30% of the current guesses. "Divisible by n" is a powerup that tells the player whether the number is divisible by a certain number. The powerup only provides numbers within 0 to 10. For example, if the secret number is 64, then the powerup should return a sentence. An example would be: "The secret number is divisible by 8, 4, and 2." "Even/Odd" is just a low-tier powerup that tells the player whether the secret number is an even or an odd.
  • Strategies: It is important to remember that this game is base on luck, and players can play it several times. If the player gets a streak right at the beginning level, it is a good idea to use a powerup during this time to increase the chances of guessing the answer and get a score multiplier by a certain amount for the streak.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: It is a dark world where people endowed with luck can easily reach the top. Blessings are provided to them by the divine providence as they go through life. However, not everyone is lucky. The world is in chaos; poverty and homelessness are all over the place. Some hope and dream of the day that will bring them great luck, some gave up and die, but some are brave enough to keep going and strive to move up the ladder. Living is hard, but that is life. People must understand that everyone starts somewhere, and with enough perseverance and patience, they will eventually reach their goal in life.
