  • High Concept: A maze game that speeds up the monsters as the level go up.
  • Theme: The theme for this game is all about speed and instensity. The more the player goes up a level, the harder it is to score points.
  • Mandated Variety: In terms of playstyle, the player can move to a certain location or place a block to block the enemies from killing the character. The randomness is applied in how many enemies respawn at a certain level.
  • Prototype Goal: Fast-thinking and stress-inducing
  • Player Experience Goals: Intense, rage, and frustration
  • Gameplay: Players can move around the area where it's not blocked. The player can use block to either kill monster/s or use it to block them. There's only one goal in this game: reach the escape region without getting hit by the raging monsters.
  • Strategies: Play it calmly and use blocks wisely.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: None
  • Target Audience: Everyone
  • Play Time: Indefinite
